Thesis 1: Year-End Momentum

Our first thesis is based on historical performance following strong starts. In the 8 years since 2006 where SPY was up >10% by October's end, the market has *always* finished positive. The retail sector (XRT) has shown even stronger average returns in those same periods.

Average Rest-of-Year (Nov-Dec) Return when SPY YTD > 10%

Thesis 2: The Retail (XRT) "Catch-Up" Trade

This is our core tactical thesis. The current setup (SPY +16.7% vs. XRT +2.1%) mirrors a specific, rare historical pattern. In years with a strong market but lagging retail, the retail sector has historically "caught up" with significant outperformance in the final two months.

Performance gap in historical "catch-up" years vs. 2025.

Our Proposed Strategy

Based on this analysis, we will adopt a bullish stance with a specific tactical tilt toward the retail sector to capture both broad market momentum and the anticipated "catch-up" rally in XRT.
