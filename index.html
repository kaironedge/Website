Another Potential Cause: Yen Carry Trade Unwind (again)

The Dynamic

Japan's massive fiscal stimulus is pushing domestic bond yields higher (breaking 1.7%).

The Flow: Investors are selling US Dollar assets (Tech stocks, Crypto) to repatriate capital back to Japan to earn these new, higher risk-free rates.
